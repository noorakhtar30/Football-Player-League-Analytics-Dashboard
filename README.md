## Data Source
Public football dataset containing structured data on:
- Player attributes (age, position, overall rating)
- Club and league affiliations
- Country-wise player representation
- Performance ratings and distributions

## Business Problem
Football analysts and decision-makers often lack a clear way to:
- Compare player performance across leagues and countries  
- Understand age and rating distributions at league or club level  
- Identify talent concentration by region  

Raw football datasets make these insights difficult to interpret without visual analytics.

## Dashboard Goals
- Analyze player ratings and demographics across leagues  
- Compare clubs, leagues, and countries using key performance metrics  
- Support scouting, performance analysis, and league benchmarking  

## Key Features
- **KPI Cards:** Total players, average ratings, leagues, and countries  
- **League Analysis:** Player distribution and rating trends by league  
- **Club Analysis:** Club-wise player counts and performance metrics  
- **Age Distribution:** Player age trends across leagues  
- **Geographic Insights:** Country-wise player representation  

## Business Impact
- **Scouting Insights:** Identify high-performing leagues and talent pools  
- **Performance Analysis:** Compare player quality across clubs and regions  
- **Strategic Decisions:** Support recruitment and league benchmarking

- Power BI dashboard design
